Meet Santa
for the first time

Today, I meet Santa for the first time. He was so big and chatful, asked me so many questions to make sure I have been a good kids in 2020. He was wearing a mask and we have a piece of glass between us, so I couldn't see his face and touch him. At the end though, he did give a Chirstmas present, so I guess I have been a good kid after all.
The place that lead to to Santa's house was very pretty too, I want to go visit him again next year!
