22nd Nov, 2020

Karamatua Lopp
Tack walk

Sunday was a such nice day, I couldn’t resist the beautiful weather outside, I decide not to have my usual nap after lunch, so daddy and mommy pack all my bags and went out again for a hike in the Waitakere range to enjoy the sunny Sunday afternoon, this time we decide to explore the Karamatua loop track in Huia.

We track along the water steam and native kauri tree for a while, until we arrived in the beautiful Karamatura falls, the water is so clear, and the breezing air is so refreshing after the long hot walk. We continue our loop after a few pictures taken, on the way back, we stop by a lookout to enjoy the gorgeous green scenary of the regional park.
The track along the shade was so pleasant and I was so tired, quickly I fall asleep at daddy’s back during our way back, I should have had my nap, luckly I did’t miss too much. Hopefully next week there will be nice weather again so daddy and mommy can take me out again.
