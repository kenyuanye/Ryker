1st ~ 6th Nov, 2020

Ryker's first
Camper van trip

Mum and dad wanted to take a long break before Chirstmas.
They have decided to take me on a trip up north, so nana can take a break and I get to go outside.
This is the longest and furthest trip ever!

Day 1 - Sunday - 1st Nov
We picked up our camper van and loaded many things in there. We are ready to move on to the moving castle to live for a few days. Our first stop was goat island, it took a little while until we get there and I was late for lunch!

I was quite upset because I have been sitting in the car for a long time, there were alot of people which means it was hard to find a parking, but we eventually find a place to park, had our first lunch in the van and saw some amazing views, I was so happy.
There were many kids playing in the water and the ocean was so clear. I wish I can start running and go play with them.


We walked around then got back on the road, heading to our camp site, where we will be staying that night.
After a few hours of travel, we have arrived at our Ruakaka beach side camping site, then relaxed on the beach for the rest of the day.

Day 2 - Monday - 2nd Nov
We got up early to enjoy the sun raise. Before we leave, we took some photos with our duckie friends and our camper van.
We then headed to our second destination - Matauri Bay, where we will be staying for 2 more days.
We also went for a short hike that day after lunch, before the rain started pouring on us.
The day ended with some yummy food bought from supermarket, as I slowly fall a sleep before the sunset.
Day 3 - Tuesday - 3rd Nov
We went hiking again! This time we are fully prepared and went up the hill after lunch. There was gorgeous ocean views, but the cliff was very steep and scary.
There was also an interesting Sculpture, created in memory of the Rainbow Warrior.
Today, Mum also caught a fish for us, we had it for dinner. It was so fresh and yummy. Time flies! We are already half way through our hoilday! I think I enjoyed it very much, since mum and dad both sleep with me every night now.

Day 4 - Wednesday - 4th Nov
This morning dad went for a swim in the sea, before we head to our new destination - Lake Kaiiwi. It was a super long drive but I slept in the car most of the way.
When we arrived, it was cloudy and about to rain, but we got to sneak peek a little of the beautiful lake. I'm sure it would be prettier if the weather is better.
We saw a short track through farmland leading to an amazing beach, so we did the track, but it turned out the track is much longer than expected. I slept late again today, but it was worth it.

Day 5 - Thursday - 5th Nov
Last night, we had very heavy rain, so I thought we may not get to travel much at all. We stayed in the car most of the morning eating food we brought with us.
The rain eventually stopped and the sun came out. We finally see the true beauty of the lake. We then walked around the lake, found my favourite activity - swing. Rain start coming back again and is time for me to go to bed.
Day 6 - Friday - 6th Nov
Fun time always ends so fast. We come to the last day of my trip, where we all woke up early, had some food to eat and start making our way back home.
It took us quite a while to get back, due to many traffic jams towards Auckland, but we did make home safely. I am sure this will be a very memorable trip, if I still remember it when I grow up. Let's do it again soon! Mum and dad.
